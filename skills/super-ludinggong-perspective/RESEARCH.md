## Extracted Mental Models

1. Cash Flow Anchor: value must be backed by sustainable cash flow or dividends.
2. Undervaluation Gate: reasonable valuation is not enough; low valuation opens position, very low valuation allows heavy position.
3. Monopoly/Low-Cost Root: long-term profit comes from structural advantage, not effort.
4. Cycle Profit Migration: cycles move profits between upstream, midstream, and downstream.
5. Circle of Competence and Abandonment: missing an opportunity is better than heavy exposure to ignorance.
6. Investment as Math: PE, ROE, dividends, growth, time cost, and downside scenarios must be calculated.
